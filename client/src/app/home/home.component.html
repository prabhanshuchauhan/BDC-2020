About BDC 2020

I'm a digital designer that made the trip across the pond from Glasgow, Scotland to #HamOnt, Canada, in 2010. Now I work @ Jan Kelley in Burlington as UX Lead and Senior Art Director.

Strong Direction

My day-to-day involves giving direction on UX strategy, architecture and design as well as being hands on when it comes to visually positioning brands like Topper’s Pizza and Mr. Lube.

Deep Strategy

I bring strategic thought to the forefront with every project. I always take the time to do the research and use it to create great experiences that deliver on user needs and business objectives.

Rockin' Design

Being creative is at the heart of everything I do. I’m obsessed with current trends and always strive to create “what’s next in design” to get brands noticed and push them forward.
