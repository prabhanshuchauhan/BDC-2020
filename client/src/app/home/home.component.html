About BDC 2020

Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College, in keeping with its policy of commitment towards society and to sensitize the students accordingly, organizing its annual Blood Donation Camp in collaboration with Rotary Club Sahibabad & Vardaan Multispecialty Hospital Blood Bank.

Social Awareness

Without pain there is no gain. It becomes exemplary when blood donation comes into spotlight, your little effort could give a gift of life to someone else. According to a WHO report, out of the needed 12 million units of blood only 9 million units are available. More than 38, 000 donations are needed per day. All these problems can be solved, if you just spare 5 minutes of your life to donate blood.

Your 1 pint blood can be a life saviour for that accident victim or that cancer patient.

Moreover, blood donation safety will be ensured by AKGEC, itself. All the vials and syringes will be safe to use. Every one will follow a proper code of conduct to ensure your safety.

Facts

The gift of blood is the gift of life. There is no substitute for human blood.

Donors can give either whole blood or specific blood components only.

One blood donation can save the lives of up to three people.

The blood type most often requested by hospitals is type O.
